# Spike Ticket Template

## Context

Why this research is needed now.

## Question / Hypothesis

Primary unknown to resolve.

## Time Box

Bounded effort (for example, 2 dev-days or 1 sprint).

## Success Criteria

- Decision recommendation with tradeoffs.
- Evidence to support next-step direction.

## Out of Scope

What this spike does not implement.

## Deliverables

- Written findings location.
- Follow-up tickets to create/update.

## Links

- Related PRDs, ADRs, RFCs, and issues.
